<p><strong>The Axles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Live</li><li>Dead</li><li>Tandem Axles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axles play a crucial role in vehicle performance and stability, categorized into three main types. Live axles actively receive power from the engine, driving the wheels and enhancing traction. Dead axles, in contrast, do not transmit power but support weight, often found in trailers. Tandem axles consist of two axles closely spaced to distribute weight more evenly, improving load capacity and stability. Each type serves specific functions, influencing vehicle design, efficiency, and suitability for various applications.</p></p>
