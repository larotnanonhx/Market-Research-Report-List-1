<p><p>The Mill Hydraulic Cylinders market comprises various types, including single acting, double acting, and multi-stage/tandem cylinders. Single acting cylinders use pressure to move in one direction and return by gravity or spring force. Double acting cylinders function with pressure applied in both directions, providing greater force and control. Multi-stage or tandem cylinders consist of multiple piston stages that increase force output in a compact design, suitable for applications requiring significant lifting capacity and efficiency in constrained spaces.</p></p>
