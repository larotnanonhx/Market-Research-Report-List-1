<p><p>Magnetic levitation stirrers utilize magnetic fields to facilitate mixing without physical contact. The market is segmented into two primary types: top entry and bottom entry. Top entry stirrers have a magnetic drive shaft positioned above the vessel, ideal for standard lab applications where easy access is required. In contrast, bottom entry stirrers feature the magnetic mechanism below the container, providing more efficient mixing for high-viscosity fluids and minimizing contamination risks, making them suitable for specialized industrial processes.</p></p>
