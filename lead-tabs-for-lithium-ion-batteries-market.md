<p><p>Lead tabs in lithium-ion batteries are crucial components that connect the battery cells to the external circuitry. The market for lead tabs is primarily categorized into three types: aluminum, nickel-plated copper, and pure nickel. Aluminum tabs are lightweight and offer good conductivity at a lower cost but may corrode over time. Nickel-plated copper tabs provide excellent conductivity and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Pure nickel tabs are known for their durability and high conductivity, ensuring reliable battery performance.</p></p>
