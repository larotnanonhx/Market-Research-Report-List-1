<p><strong>The Solar Control Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbing Solar Control Glass</li><li>Reflective Solar Control Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar control glass market comprises two primary types: absorbing solar control glass and reflective solar control glass. Absorbing solar control glass is designed to minimize solar heat gain by absorbing sunlight, thereby reducing glare and improving interior comfort. In contrast, reflective solar control glass features a coating that reflects a significant portion of solar radiation, keeping interiors cooler while enhancing privacy. Both types are essential for energy-efficient buildings, enabling better climate control and reduced reliance on air conditioning systems.</p></p>
