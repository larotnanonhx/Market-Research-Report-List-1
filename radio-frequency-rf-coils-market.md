<p><p>The Radio Frequency (RF) coils market comprises various types, notably volume coils and surface coils. Volume coils are designed to capture signals from larger areas, providing uniform coverage and deeper tissue imaging, making them ideal for whole-body scans. In contrast, surface coils are smaller, targeting specific body parts for higher resolution and improved sensitivity. Both types play crucial roles in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), catering to diverse clinical needs and enhancing diagnostic capabilities in medical imaging.</p></p>
