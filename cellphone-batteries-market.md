<p><ul><li>Nickel-cadmium Battery</li><li>Nickel-hydrogen Battery</li><li>Lithium-ion Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellphone battery market comprises several types, including nickel-cadmium (NiCd), nickel-hydrogen (NiMH), and lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries. NiCd batteries, known for their durability and resistance to extreme temperatures, have largely fallen out of favor due to memory effect and environmental concerns. NiMH batteries offer better capacity and are less toxic but have limited lifespan. In contrast, Li-ion batteries dominate the market due to their high energy density, lightweight design, and longer life cycles, making them ideal for modern smartphones.</p></p>
