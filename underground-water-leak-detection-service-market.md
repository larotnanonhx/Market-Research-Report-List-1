<p><p>The underground water leak detection service market encompasses two primary types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves physically accessing pipelines to identify leaks, which may require excavation or disruption of surrounding areas. Conversely, non-invasive leak detection utilizes advanced technology, such as acoustic sensors and thermal imaging, to locate leaks without causing damage to the infrastructure. Both methods cater to different customer needs, balancing accuracy, cost, and potential disruption to the environment or property.</p></p>
