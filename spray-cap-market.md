<p><p>Spray caps are specialized nozzles used in aerosol cans, designed for different spray patterns and applications. Fat caps produce a wide spray for broad coverage, ideal for filling large areas quickly. Tighter or thin caps create a narrow, precise spray, perfect for detailing and intricate work. Medium spray caps offer a balanced spray width, suitable for general use, blending the features of both fat and thin caps. Each type caters to specific needs in painting, graffiti, and other spray applications.</p></p>
