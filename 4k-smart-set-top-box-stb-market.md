<p><p>The 4K Smart Set-Top Box (STB) market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **IPTV (Internet Protocol Television)** delivers content via internet connections, enabling high-quality streams.</p><p>2. **Satellite STBs** receive signals from satellites, offering wide coverage and premium channels.</p><p>3. **DTT (Digital Terrestrial Television)** transmits over-the-air signals, providing accessible broadcasting.</p><p>4. **OTT (Over-The-Top)** services stream content directly over the internet, bypassing traditional platforms. </p><p>5. **Hybrid STBs** combine multiple technologies, offering versatility by accessing both internet and broadcast channels.</p></p>
