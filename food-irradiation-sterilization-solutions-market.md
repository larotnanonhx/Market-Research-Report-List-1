<p><p>The food irradiation sterilization solutions market encompasses three primary technologies: gamma radiation, X-ray radiation, and electron beam radiation. Gamma radiation uses radioactive isotopes to penetrate and kill microorganisms, extending shelf life. X-ray radiation offers deeper penetration and increased efficiency, effectively reducing pathogens while preserving food quality. Electron beam radiation uses high-energy electrons to rapidly sterilize food with minimal impact on temperature and nutrients. These methods enhance food safety, reduce waste, and meet growing consumer demand for preserved yet nutritious products.</p></p>
