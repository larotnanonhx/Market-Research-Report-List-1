<p><p>The OLED TCON (Timing Control) chips market is categorized into two main types: Independent TCON chips and Integrated TCON chips. Independent TCON chips function as standalone components, managing display timing and synchronization separately from other circuitry, allowing for greater flexibility in design and performance. In contrast, Integrated TCON chips combine timing control with other functionalities within a single chip, reducing the overall size and cost, while streamlining the manufacturing process. Each type serves distinct applications based on design requirements.</p></p>
