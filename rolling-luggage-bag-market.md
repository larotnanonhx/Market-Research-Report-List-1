<p><p>The rolling luggage bag market is primarily segmented into two types: 2-wheel and 4-wheel bags. 2-wheel bags feature a traditional design with two wheels, requiring users to tilt the bag for maneuverability, which can be easier on uneven surfaces. In contrast, 4-wheel bags have four wheels that rotate 360 degrees, allowing for easier navigation in tight spaces and smoother rolling on flat surfaces. The choice between the two often depends on user preference, travel needs, and terrain considerations.</p></p>
