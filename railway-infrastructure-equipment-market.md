<p><strong>The Railway Infrastructure Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-Structure</li><li>Super Structure</li><li>Special Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway infrastructure equipment market is categorized into three main types: sub-structure, superstructure, and special structure. </p><p>Sub-structure includes foundational elements like ballast, sleepers, and track foundations that ensure stability and support for the railway line. </p><p>Superstructure refers to the components directly in contact with trains, such as tracks, rail joints, and ties, enabling smooth train operations. </p><p>Special structures pertain to unique installations like bridges, tunnels, and level crossings that accommodate specific terrain challenges and operational requirements in the railway network.</p></p>
