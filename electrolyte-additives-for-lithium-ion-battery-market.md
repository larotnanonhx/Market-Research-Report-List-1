<p><p>Electrolyte additives enhance lithium-ion battery performance and stability. Vinylene carbonate (VC) forms a robust solid-electrolyte interface, improving lifespan. Fluorinated ethylene carbonate (FEC) enhances thermal stability and reduces gas evolution. 1,3-Propane sultone (1,3-PS) promotes high-voltage stability while minimizing electrolyte decomposition. Vinyl ethylene carbonate (VEC) improves cycle life by forming a protective layer. Lithium bis(fluorosulfonyl)imide (LiFSI) enhances ionic conductivity and thermal stability. Other additives may include proprietary compounds designed to optimize specific electrochemical properties or performance outcomes.</p></p>
