<p><strong>The Electric Fire Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Single Stage Fire Pump</li><li>Electric Multi Stage Fire Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric fire pump market comprises primarily two types: electric single stage fire pumps and electric multi-stage fire pumps. Electric single stage fire pumps utilize a single impeller to generate pressure, making them ideal for low-rise buildings and applications requiring moderate flow rates. In contrast, electric multi-stage fire pumps feature multiple impellers, enabling them to achieve higher pressures suitable for taller structures or scenarios demanding elevated flow rates. Both types are essential for effective fire suppression systems.</p></p>
