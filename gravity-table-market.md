<p><p>Gravity table market encompasses various separation technologies based on gravitational forces to efficiently separate materials based on their density and particle size. Multi-gravity separation utilizes enhanced gravitational effects to improve fine particle recovery. Jigging separation employs periodic motion to segregate materials by weight, allowing for effective mineral processing. Shaking tables further refine this process by creating a controlled, oscillating motion that promotes the separation of heavier particles from lighter ones, optimizing mineral extraction and concentration. Each method plays a vital role in the mining and recycling industries.</p></p>
