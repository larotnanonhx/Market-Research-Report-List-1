<p><strong>The Automated External Defibrillator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-automated</li><li>Fully automated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated External Defibrillator (AED) market is segmented into semi-automated and fully automated devices. Semi-automated AEDs require some user intervention, prompting the operator to deliver a shock after analyzing the heart rhythm, making them suitable for trained bystanders. In contrast, fully automated AEDs deliver shocks without requiring user action, making them ideal for untrained individuals. Both types aim to enhance cardiac arrest survival rates by providing timely defibrillation, but they cater to different user expertise levels.</p></p>
