<p><p>The sand washing and recycling machine market includes two primary types: wheel sand washing machines and screw sand washing machines. Wheel sand washing machines utilize a circular rotor to remove impurities and produce cleaner sand, making them ideal for applications requiring high purity. Screw sand washing machines, on the other hand, use a helical screw mechanism to separate sand from water, effectively recycling and managing sand while efficiently removing contaminants. Both types enhance sand quality and optimize resource management in construction and mining industries.</p></p>
