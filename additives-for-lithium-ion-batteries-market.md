<p><p>The Lithium-ion battery additives market is divided into organic and inorganic types. Inorganic additives, including metal oxides and salts, boost thermal stability, increase capacity, and improve safety by mitigating risks of overheating. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing battery efficiency, longevity, and overall safety, essential for various applications in electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
