<p><p>The light industrial conveyor belts market is primarily categorized by two manufacturing processes: coating and calendering. The coating process involves applying a layer of material onto a belt substrate to enhance properties like durability and resistance to chemicals. In contrast, the calendering process involves passing materials through rollers to achieve desired thickness and texture, ensuring uniformity. Both methods significantly impact the performance, flexibility, and longevity of the conveyor belts, catering to diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
