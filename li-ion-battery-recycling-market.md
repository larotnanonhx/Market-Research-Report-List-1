NMC (Nickel Manganese Cobalt) batteries, favored in electric vehicles, have complex recycling needs due to their mixed metal content. LiFePO4 batteries, known for safety and longevity, offer easier recycling processes but still require specialized methods. Other market segments may include batteries from consumer electronics and renewable energy storage, each with unique recycling requirements.</p></p>
