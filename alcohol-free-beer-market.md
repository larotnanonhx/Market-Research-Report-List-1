<p><strong>The Alcohol free Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fermentation-limited Method</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol-free beer market is primarily divided into two types: fermentation-limited and dealcoholization methods. The fermentation-limited method involves restricting fermentation to minimize alcohol production while retaining flavors, resulting in a beer that is low in alcohol. In contrast, the dealcoholization method starts with regular beer, which is then processed to remove alcohol, often using techniques like reverse osmosis or evaporation. Both methods aim to provide a beer-like experience without the intoxicating effects, catering to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
