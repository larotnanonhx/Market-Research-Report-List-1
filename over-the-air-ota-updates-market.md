<p><p>Over-the-Air (OTA) updates encompass two primary market types: Firmware over-the-Air (FOTA) and Software over-the-Air (SOTA). FOTA pertains to the wireless delivery of firmware updates to devices, enhancing performance, security, and functionality without user intervention. SOTA, on the other hand, focuses on software applications and their updates, ensuring users have access to the latest features and bug fixes. Both markets facilitate seamless upgrades for devices, promoting user satisfaction and device longevity across various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and IoT.</p></p>
