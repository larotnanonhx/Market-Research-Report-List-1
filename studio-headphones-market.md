<p><strong>The Studio Headphones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Back</li><li>Semi-Open Back</li><li>Fully-Open Back</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The studio headphones market is categorized into three main types: closed back, semi-open back, and fully-open back. Closed back headphones provide superior sound isolation, making them ideal for recording environments by preventing sound leakage. Semi-open back headphones offer a balance, allowing some ambient sound while still maintaining good isolation, suitable for mixing and critical listening. Fully-open back headphones produce a broader soundstage for a more natural listening experience, perfect for detailed audio analysis, though they lack isolation and are less suitable for noisy environments.</p></p>
