<p><p>The electrosurgical knife market includes insulation-tipped (IT) knives and needle-tipped knives, each designed for specific surgical applications. IT knives have an insulated shaft, allowing precision cutting while minimizing thermal damage to surrounding tissues, making them ideal for delicate procedures. Needle-tipped knives feature a sharp, pointed electrode that offers precise incision capabilities, suitable for tasks requiring fine cutting and coagulation. Both types enhance surgical efficiency, reduce bleeding, and improve patient outcomes, driving their demand in operating rooms.</p></p>
