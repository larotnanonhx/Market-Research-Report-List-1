<p><strong>The Swellable Packers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Packers</li><li>Retrievable Packers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swellable packers market is categorized into two primary types: permanent packers and retrievable packers. Permanent packers are designed for long-term applications, where they form a seal with the formation fluids, remaining in place throughout the well's lifecycle. In contrast, retrievable packers are engineered for temporary use, allowing for easy removal and reinstallation in multiple locations. Both types are essential in various oil and gas applications for enhancing well integrity, isolating zones, and enabling efficient production operations.</p></p>
