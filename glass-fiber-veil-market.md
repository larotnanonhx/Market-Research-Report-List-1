<p><strong>The Glass Fiber Veil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet-Laid Process</li><li>Dry-Laid Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Veil market encompasses two primary production processes: wet-laid and dry-laid. The wet-laid process involves depositing glass fibers onto a wet substrate, allowing for the bonding of fibers with a liquid medium, enhancing flexibility and uniformity. In contrast, the dry-laid process utilizes dry fibers that are laid onto a surface and bonded through mechanical or thermal methods, providing a denser and more rigid structure. Both methods serve diverse applications in construction, automotive, and composites industries, offering unique advantages.</p></p>
