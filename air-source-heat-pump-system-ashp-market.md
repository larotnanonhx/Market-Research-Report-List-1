<p><p>Air-Source Heat Pump (ASHP) systems extract thermal energy from the outside air, providing both heating and cooling solutions. The market for ASHPs is primarily divided into two types: Air-to-Air and Air-to-Water. Air-to-Air systems transfer heat directly to the indoor air, ideal for residential heating and cooling. In contrast, Air-to-Water systems use the extracted heat to warm water for radiators or underfloor heating, making them suitable for both residential and commercial applications where hot water is needed.</p></p>
