<p><p>Distraction osteogenesis devices are essential in orthopedics and reconstructive surgery, facilitating bone regeneration. The market comprises two main types: internal distractors and external distractors. Internal distractors are surgically implanted within the bone and allow for gradual distraction from within, providing a more discreet solution with reduced external visibility. In contrast, external distractors are attached externally, enabling the adjustment of bone lengthening from outside the body. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
