<p><p>The Fire Fighting Foam Concentrate market is categorized primarily into Class A and Class B foams. Class A foam is designed for structural fires and is effective on ordinary combustibles such as wood and paper, enhancing water's effectiveness and reducing surface tension. In contrast, Class B foam targets flammable liquids and is used for fires involving fuels, oils, and solvents, forming a barrier to smother flames and prevent re-ignition. Both types are essential for diverse firefighting applications.</p></p>
