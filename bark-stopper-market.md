<p><p>Bark stoppers are designed to reduce unwanted barking in dogs through various methods. Liquid (spray) bark stoppers use citronella or similar scents released when a dog barks, deterring them from continuing. Electric bark stoppers deliver a mild static shock when barking is detected, discouraging the behavior. Ultrasonic bark stoppers emit high-frequency sounds that are uncomfortable for dogs but inaudible to humans, helping to curb excessive barking. Each type targets the issue effectively, appealing to different dog owners' preferences.</p></p>
