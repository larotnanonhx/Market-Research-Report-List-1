<p><p>The plastic rectifier market can be categorized based on key performance parameters. Maximum reverse current indicates the highest reverse leakage current a rectifier can handle without failure. Maximum peak current reflects the utmost peak load the rectifier can endure momentarily. Maximum reverse voltage signifies the maximum voltage the device can withstand in the reverse direction. Forward voltage represents the voltage drop across the rectifier when conducting, while maximum average rectified current denotes the continuous load it can deliver safely. These metrics guide selection and application in various electronic systems.</p></p>
