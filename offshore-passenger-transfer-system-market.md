<p><p>The Offshore Passenger Transfer System market encompasses two main types: Large Transfer Systems and Small Transfer Systems. Large Transfer Systems are designed to accommodate high-capacity transfers, catering to multiple passengers simultaneously, often used in extensive offshore operations like oil rigs. Both systems prioritize safety and efficiency, adapting to various operational needs within the offshore industry.</p></p>
